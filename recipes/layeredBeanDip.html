Layered Bean Dip

Home Page

A delicious bean layered dip perfect for tacos or as dip for nachos. 20 minute prep time, 1 hour cook ti,e total 1 hour and 20 minutes. Serves 32 and yields approximately 4 cups.

Ingredients you'll need

  • 1 (8 oucne) package creamed cheese, softened
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 1 cup cocitail sauce
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted and diced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 bunch green onions, chopped
  • 1 (2 ounce) can black olives, drained and chopped
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded cheddar cheese

How to make the layered Bean Dip

  1. In a small bowl, mix the cream cheese and milk until smooth.
  2. Spread onto a large, round serving dish.
  3. Layer with cocktail sauce.
  4. Top with avocado.
  5. Layer with tomato, green bell pepper, green onions and black olives.
  6. Sprinkle with cheese.
  7. Chill in the refrigerator 1 hour, or until firm
